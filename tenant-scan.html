Why This Matters

The Three Risks Hiding in Your Tenant

Silent governance gaps that only surface under audit pressure — or when a security incident forces your hand.

🌀

Workspace Sprawl

The average enterprise has 300–500+ workspaces. Our scans typically find 30–40% are unowned or redundant. Without visibility, you're migrating dead weight into Fabric — and paying for it.

🔓

Access Pattern Drift

Typical audits reveal 25–30% of workspace access is orphaned or over-provisioned. Former team members, legacy service accounts, and stale permissions create compliance exposure that's invisible until SOX or ISO 27001 forces your hand.

💰

License & Capacity Waste

Most tenants overspend 30–45% on Pro licenses assigned to inactive users. Add unused Premium capacity and redundant datasets, and you're burning budget that could fund your Fabric migration.
