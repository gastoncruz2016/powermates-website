Power Platform February 28, 2026

Power Platform February 2026 Feature Update

A massive month for Power Platform — Code Apps hit GA, Copilot lands inside model-driven apps, and the new Power Apps MCP Server opens the door to agent-driven business applications.

💻
Code Apps — Now Generally Available

Code apps in Power Apps are now GA, giving developers full code-first flexibility with React, Vue, and other frameworks while IT gets enterprise-grade guardrails. Build in any code-first IDE, deploy to Power Apps — bridging the gap between pro-dev and citizen development.

🤖
M365 Copilot Chat in Model-Driven Apps

Copilot chat is now available directly inside model-driven apps. Users can ask questions, reason over in-app data, and connect insights from documents and communications — all without leaving the application. A unified AI experience embedded in the flow of business.

🔌
Power Apps MCP Server — Public Preview

The new Model Context Protocol (MCP) Server enables AI agents to automate repetitive app tasks with human review and approval. The enhanced agent feed provides built-in supervision tools so humans can review, assist, and take control of agent actions when needed.

Read the full February 2026 update on Power Platform Blog
AI & Agents February 24, 2026

The Shift Reshaping Enterprise Applications: Agent-First Solutions

Microsoft outlines the dramatic expansion in what's possible to build with AI and agents — and why the people who understand finance, HR, supply chain, and customer service need to be involved in shaping these systems.

🏢
Agent-First, Not Code-First

The enterprise application landscape is shifting from traditional form-based apps to agent-driven solutions where AI handles routine tasks, surfaces insights proactively, and orchestrates workflows across systems. Power Platform is positioning itself at the center of this shift.

👥
Business Users as Solution Shapers

Companies that want AI to meaningfully change operations need domain experts — not just developers — shaping the systems they rely on. Power Platform's low-code approach enables finance, HR, and operations teams to build and customize agent-powered solutions directly.

⚡
Copilot Studio + Power Apps + Dataverse

The convergence of Copilot Studio for agent creation, Power Apps for business interfaces, and Dataverse for structured data creates a complete stack for building enterprise-grade AI solutions — with governance, security, and compliance built in from the start.

Microsoft Fabric February 25, 2026

Fabric February 2026 Feature Summary

A wide range of enhancements land across the Fabric platform this month — from OneLake Catalog improvements and developer experiences to meaningful updates in Data Engineering, Data Factory, and Real-Time Intelligence.

🗂️
OneLake Catalog

Workspace Apps are now included in the OneLake Catalog, making it the single place to discover all Fabric content. The updated Item Details page delivers a consistent, modern design with richer metadata and full schema visibility.

⚡
Data Engineering — New ODBC Driver

A brand-new ODBC Driver built for Fabric Data Engineering offers deep OneLake integration, environment-based execution, ODBC 3.x compliance, and Microsoft Entra ID authentication — accelerating Spark-powered data engineering at scale.

🔁
Real-Time Intelligence & Data Factory

February also brings improvements to Real-Time Intelligence pipelines and Data Factory connectors. With FabCon Atlanta just weeks away (March 16–20), these updates are a strong preview of the bigger announcements coming live at the conference.
