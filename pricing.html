FAQ

Common Questions

How does the engagement work?
We start with a 30-minute discovery call to understand your environment. After scope confirmation, we request read-only access to your Fabric tenant and deliver results within the stated timeline.
Do you need admin access?
Most audits require read-only access. We'll specify exact permissions needed during the discovery call.
Can I start small and expand?
Absolutely. Many clients start with a Semantic Model Audit ($2,500) and expand to a full Tenant Scan or bundle based on initial findings.
What if I need something custom?
Contact us. We regularly design custom engagements for enterprises with specific requirements.
How do bundles work?
Bundles combine two or more individual services at a discount. You get the full scope of each included service, delivered as a coordinated engagement with a single timeline and report.
