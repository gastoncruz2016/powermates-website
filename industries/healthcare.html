Multi-Facility Health System Accelerates Clinical Reporting

A 15-hospital regional health system needed HIPAA-compliant dashboards for patient outcomes, clinical trial management, and quality metrics across all facilities. Their legacy BI infrastructure couldn't aggregate data securely or fast enough to meet clinicians' needs.

We designed a Fabric workspace structure with fine-grained role-based access control, built semantic models for clinical analytics, and deployed Power BI reports across all 15 facilities. Every dashboard met HIPAA requirements with automatic data masking for sensitive fields.

Result: 40% faster reporting cycle (from 3 days to 18 hours). Zero compliance issues in subsequent audit. Clinicians now access real-time outcome data without IT intervention.
