A regional investment firm had 15+ disconnected systems for risk reporting, trading dashboards, and regulatory compliance. Data was siloed across SQL Server, legacy BI tools, and spreadsheets. Regulatory reports took 3+ days to compile manually.

We architected a medallion lakehouse in Microsoft Fabric, consolidated financial data from multiple sources, and built automated regulatory and risk dashboards. Every report now meets SOC 2 audit requirements with full data lineage and access controls.

Result: 60% faster report generation (3 days to 18 hours). OneLake migration completed without data loss. Real-time risk dashboards enable faster decision-making. Zero compliance violations in subsequent audit.
