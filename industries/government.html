Federal Agency Consolidates 200+ Data Sources & Passes FedRAMP Audit

A federal agency had intelligence data scattered across 200+ legacy systems, Excel spreadsheets, and isolated databases. No unified view of mission performance existed. Multiple FedRAMP audits identified gaps in data governance, security controls, and audit trails. The agency needed to consolidate data securely while maintaining classification boundaries.

We architected a secure Fabric lakehouse with row-level security, implemented compartmented workspaces for different clearance levels, and built Power BI dashboards visible only to authorized personnel. All 200+ data sources now feed into a unified, compliant platform with complete audit trails.

Result: 70% faster compliance reporting. Zero FedRAMP audit findings on analytics. 200+ data sources consolidated into one secure platform. Analysts now access mission data in hours instead of weeks.
