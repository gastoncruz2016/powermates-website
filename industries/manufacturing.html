Global Manufacturer Achieves Real-Time IoT Analytics & Cost Reduction

A global manufacturer with 8 facilities across 4 continents needed real-time visibility into OEE, quality metrics, and supply chain operations. Existing systems couldn't ingest IoT data fast enough or provide actionable dashboards. Facility managers had no visibility across plants.

We built a Fabric lakehouse that ingests IoT telemetry from 5,000+ sensors via Eventstreams, performs real-time analysis in KQL, and powers Power BI dashboards across all 8 facilities. Every plant now has live OEE, downtime alerts, and quality dashboards.

Result: 25% reduction in unplanned downtime, 18% improvement in OEE across all facilities. Real-time alerting enabled immediate response to equipment failures. Supply chain visibility prevented $2.3M in emergency shipments. ROI achieved in 6 months.
