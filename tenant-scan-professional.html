A Power BI tenant governance scan isn't just an audit — it's a structured conversation between your data platform and your risk posture. Most organizations run their tenants reactively, discovering gaps only when compliance calls or an access incident forces action. A comprehensive tenant scan changes that: it gives you the map you've been missing — which workspaces own what, where access has drifted from reality, and what's actually ready for Fabric adoption.

The real value of a migration readiness assessment is actionability. You don't need a 50-page report that documents every workspace and dataset. You need five specific moves your delivery team can execute in the next 90 days to reduce risk and unlock opportunity. That's what the Tenant Scan delivers — the findings that matter, prioritized by impact, with concrete remediation steps and a scored baseline you can measure against over time.
