The Medallion Pipeline

A layered data architecture from raw ingestion to analytics-ready output.

DATA SOURCES  →  BRONZE (Raw)       →  SILVER (Cleaned)    →  GOLD (Analytics)    →  POWER BI
  Files            Append-only           Dedup, validate        Aggregate, curate      Direct Lake

  Databases        Partition: date       Partition: biz date     Partition: month        Reports
